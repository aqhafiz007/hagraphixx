  1. Open your Google Sheets / Excel.
  2. Auto-detect enabled! Any column order works - the system will detect headers automatically.
  3. Recommended: Sleeve | Size | Name | Collar | No/Remarks
  4. Select your data (with or without header row), press Ctrl + C.
  5. Click box below, press Ctrl + V.
